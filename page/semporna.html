Semporna is a small costal town located at the tip of Semporna Peninsula around Lahad Datu Bay (also known as Darvel Bay). The town is visited by tourists as a base for scuba diving or snorkelling trips as there are several islands with crystal clear water and exquisite coral reefs to explore. Semporna is also known as the only way to get to the widely renowned world class mythical scuba divesite, Sipadan Island.
